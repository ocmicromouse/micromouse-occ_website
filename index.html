New club, real robot, full build path

Build an autonomous maze-solving robot at Orange Coast College.

MicroMouse @ OCC helps students design, solder, program, test, and race small robots that find their way through a 16 by 16 maze. Start with a guided training bot, then design your own competition mouse from scratch.
